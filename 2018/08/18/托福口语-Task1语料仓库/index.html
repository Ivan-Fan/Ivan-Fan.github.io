文化类

  1. 你想了解一个国家/文化的原因？TPO40

    ① America

    • USA is the freest country in the world, so it must be really nice of living there, and since I want to have that kind of experience, I’d like to learn more about it first.

    • I’ll go to the postgraduate school in the USA, so it may be beneficial to understand what that country looks like or feel like, in that case, I can fit in my school life better. For example, what does the class feel like in the top school such as Stanford University or Carnegie Mellon University.

    • There’re amounts of high technologies in the USA, which inspires me a lot. Take smart phone as an example. Giant corporations like Apple, Google are really innovative, and they create various cutting-edge devices that change the whole world. That’s an imperative reason why I would like to find out how this happens.

名人类

  1. 你最敬佩哪个人？TPO41

    ① Steve Jobs

    • He was a successful American entrepreneur and business magnate, developing a line of products that had large cultural ramifications, which have changed the way of our life, such as iphone and imac.
    • Another reason is about the stories he shared during his famous Stanford commencement address back in 2005. “Keep looking and don’t settle”,as he said.It was a brief moment in his elegant speech that brought home the point that inspiration is where you find it – and that it can be anywhere if you remain open to it.

个人类

  1. 重要的accomplishment(需要投入很多经历，而且很重要)，为什么重要？ TOP42

    ① TOEFL TEST

    • It is the consideration about my self development. During the preparation for TOEFL TEST, I can enhance my ability of English, which will be useful in the international society in the future.
    • I’ll go to the postgraduate school in the USA, so it may be beneficial to get a language ability certificate. For example, I dream about studying in the top school such as Stanford University or Carnegie Mellon University, which means this accomplishment really imperative.

  2. 重要的目标 TPO43

    ① 去一个国家（仿照 文化类 美国）

    • I enjoy different ways of school life, so it’s my dream to apply to the postgraduate school in the USA. For example, what does the class feel like in the top school such as Stanford University or Carnegie Mellon University.
    • There’re amounts of high technologies in the USA, which inspires me a lot. There are Giant corporations like Apple, Google which are really innovative, and they create various cutting-edge devices that change the whole world. That’s an imperative reason why I would like to go and find out how this happens.

互联网相关

  1. 从互联网获益有哪些？TPO46

    ① 学习

    • give me access to a lot of information on the Internet like wiki-pedia and baidu search engine

      e.g have some complex math problem, I can check it from those engines above. As a result, complete process of solution can be got from the Internet, which gives me a lot of intuition

交通类

  1. 哪一种交通方式最enjoyable？bicycle，automobile，train

    ① bicycle

    • 可以看风景 enjoy sightseeing without barriers in my eyesight, and breathe fresh air
    • 可以锻炼身体
    • environment-friendly: bicycle exhausts zero emission

    • 可以看风景
