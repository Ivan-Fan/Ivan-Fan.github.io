    Task 3

    吃，住，学习，娱乐生活，校园等

    学校又有大动作了。对不起，学生们有意见

    1. 题目流程

      先是看一篇文章（30s），然后听一篇听力（1min左右）

      之后是30s整理思路，60s回答 （10s + 50s）

      听力lecture的结构如下：

    2. 模板

      announcement (plan)

      • The university is planning to do sth. /on doing sth.
        • and the m/w in the conversation agrees with this for 2 reasons. (agree)
        • however, the m/w in the conversation disagrees, holding 2 different points. (disagree) 
          The first reason is that…/ First of all, …/…
      • The second reason is that…/ …

      proposal (letter)

      • The student in the letter suggests the school should…
        • and the m/w in the conversation agrees with this for 2 reasons. (agree)
        • however, the m/w in the conversation disagrees, holding 2 different points. (disagree)
          The first reason is that…/ First of all, …/…
      • The second reason is that…/ …

    3. TIPS

      • 最好可以在看文章的时候记下所说的plan，因为文章只显示一遍

    Task 4

    心理学多一些

    lecture，教授给学生们讲故事，阐述一个term

    1. 题目流程

      先是看一篇文章（30s），然后听一篇听力（1min左右）

      之后是30s整理思路，60s回答（10s + 45s）

      听力lecture往往是一个professor在用 例子/个人经历/实验 来帮助阐述一个事情

      常听到的开头是：

      I'v experienced these things myself.

      I have an example from my own life.

    2. 模板

      • In the reading passage, there is a term called…
      • And the professor in the lecture uses (the following) as an example to help us understand.
        • his own / friends’ experience
        • an experiment
        • …(maybe an animal or plant)

    3. TIPS

      • 第一句不需要说文章中关于term的解释，重复文章中的内容没有必要

      • 一些原因，细节性的可以不说，说节点重要信息

      • 如果怕尴尬就说这句话

        This is what I learn from the passage.

    Task 5

    住行，time/schedule，conflict，paper等等

    一般是两个童鞋conversation，一个抱怨问题，另一个在安慰她帮忙想解决办法

    1. 题目流程

      听一篇听力（1min左右）

      之后是20s整理思路，60s回答（10s + 45s）

      conversation结构如下：

    2. 模板

      • The problem the m/w gets in the conversation is that…/

        The m/w speakers in the conversation get a problem that…

      • However, two possible solutions are mentioned in the conversation, one is…another is…

      • Personally, I’d recommend him/her/them to choose 1st / 2nd solution because…

        if I were the speaker in that situation, I’d definitely take the 1st / 2nd option,because…

    3. TIPS

      • 说一个的好处和另外一个的坏处

      • 听力内容少或者没记到可以自己编

    Task 6

    生物比较多

    professor给学生们讲述商业战略，动物的优劣势等等

    1. 题目流程

      听一篇听力（1min+）

      之后是20s整理思路，60s回答（10s + 45s）

      lecture结构如下：

    2. 模板

      • There are two…

        • adaptations
        • advantages
        • disadvantages
        • ways
        • strategies
        • …

      • one is …ex…

      • another is …ex…

    3. TIPS

      • lecture刚开始的废话不用管，说正事的时候开始记，说的时候要概括
      • 可以不做太多的同义转换
      • 动词连着出现，大部分都得记
      • 句式上可以采用 被动语态 + 定语从句 + 动名词ing
